Fever:
Description: A temporary increase in body temperature, often due to infection.
Symptoms: High temperature, sweating, chills, headache, muscle aches.
Treatment: Drink fluids, rest, paracetamol if needed.
Prevention: Maintain hygiene and avoid infections.
When to see doctor: If above 39°C or lasts more than 3 days.

Cough:
Description: Reflex to clear airways of mucus or irritants.
Symptoms: Dry or wet cough, throat irritation.
Treatment: Warm fluids, honey, rest.
Prevention: Avoid smoke and infections.
When to see doctor: If lasts more than 2 weeks or breathing difficulty.

Diabetes:
Description: A chronic condition affecting blood sugar levels.
Symptoms: Frequent urination, thirst, fatigue, blurred vision.
Treatment: Diet control, exercise, medication/insulin.
Prevention: Healthy lifestyle.
When to see doctor: If symptoms appear or sugar levels are high.

Hypertension:
Description: High blood pressure in arteries.
Symptoms: Often none, sometimes headache or dizziness.
Treatment: Reduce salt, exercise, medication.
Prevention: Healthy diet, stress control.
When to see doctor: If BP remains high.

Asthma:
Description: A condition where airways become inflamed and narrow.
Symptoms: Shortness of breath, wheezing, chest tightness.
Treatment: Inhalers, avoid triggers.
Prevention: Avoid allergens, pollution.
When to see doctor: If breathing becomes difficult.

Common Cold:
Description: Viral infection of nose and throat.
Symptoms: Runny nose, sneezing, sore throat.
Treatment: Rest, fluids.
Prevention: Hand hygiene.
When to see doctor: If symptoms worsen.

Flu (Influenza):
Description: Viral infection affecting respiratory system.
Symptoms: Fever, chills, body aches, fatigue.
Treatment: Rest, fluids, antiviral drugs.
Prevention: Vaccination.
When to see doctor: Severe weakness or breathing issues.

COVID-19:
Description: Infectious disease caused by coronavirus.
Symptoms: Fever, cough, loss of taste, breathing issues.
Treatment: Isolation, supportive care.
Prevention: Vaccination, masks.
When to see doctor: Breathing difficulty.

Headache:
Description: Pain in head or upper neck.
Symptoms: Pressure, throbbing pain.
Treatment: Rest, hydration, pain relievers.
Prevention: Manage stress.
When to see doctor: Severe or frequent headaches.

Migraine:
Description: Intense headache with neurological symptoms.
Symptoms: Throbbing pain, nausea, sensitivity to light.
Treatment: Medication, rest.
Prevention: Avoid triggers.
When to see doctor: Frequent migraines.

Food Poisoning:
Description: Illness from contaminated food.
Symptoms: Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea.
Treatment: Hydration, rest.
Prevention: Safe food handling.
When to see doctor: Severe dehydration.

Diarrhea:
Description: Frequent loose or watery stools.
Symptoms: Abdominal cramps, dehydration.
Treatment: ORS, fluids.
Prevention: Clean food and water.
When to see doctor: If persists >2 days.

Vomiting:
Description: Forceful expulsion of stomach contents.
Symptoms: Nausea, weakness.
Treatment: Hydration, rest.
Prevention: Avoid contaminated food.
When to see doctor: Persistent vomiting.

Allergy:
Description: Immune response to substances.
Symptoms: Sneezing, itching, rash.
Treatment: Antihistamines.
Prevention: Avoid allergens.
When to see doctor: Severe allergic reaction.

Bronchitis:
Description: Inflammation of bronchial tubes.
Symptoms: Cough with mucus, fatigue.
Treatment: Rest, fluids.
Prevention: Avoid smoking.
When to see doctor: Persistent cough.

Pneumonia:
Description: Infection that inflames air sacs in lungs.
Symptoms: Fever, cough, difficulty breathing.
Treatment: Antibiotics (if bacterial), rest.
Prevention: Vaccination.
When to see doctor: Breathing difficulty.

Heart Attack:
Description: Blockage of blood flow to heart.
Symptoms: Chest pain, shortness of breath, sweating.
Treatment: Emergency medical care.
Prevention: Healthy lifestyle.
When to see doctor: Immediately (emergency).

Stroke:
Description: Disruption of blood supply to brain.
Symptoms: Weakness, speech difficulty, confusion.
Treatment: Emergency care.
Prevention: Control BP, healthy lifestyle.
When to see doctor: Immediately (emergency).

Anxiety:
Description: Excessive worry or fear.
Symptoms: Restlessness, rapid heartbeat.
Treatment: Therapy, relaxation.
Prevention: Stress management.
When to see doctor: If affects daily life.

Depression:
Description: Mood disorder causing persistent sadness.
Symptoms: Low mood, loss of interest, sleep issues.
Treatment: Therapy, medication.
Prevention: Social support, healthy lifestyle.
When to see doctor: If symptoms persist.
